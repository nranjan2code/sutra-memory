## Executive Summary

Sutra AI's business model combines **open-source community growth** with **enterprise SaaS monetization**, targeting $50M ARR by 2030 with **Rule of 40** compliance (growth rate + profit margin ≥ 40%).

**Revenue Model:**
- Self-hosted subscriptions (60% of revenue)
- Cloud-hosted SaaS (30% of revenue)
- Professional services (10% of revenue)

**Unit Economics (Steady State):**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): $5,000
- LTV (Lifetime Value): $250,000
- LTV:CAC ratio: 50:1 (exceptional)
- Payback period: 3 months
- Gross margin: 85%+

**Funding Need:** $5-8M Series A (Q2 2026) to scale GTM

## Unit Economics Analysis

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**CAC Components:**
```
Marketing Spend (per customer):
- Paid ads: $1,000 (50 clicks @ $20, 2% conversion)
- Content/SEO: $500 (allocated)
- Events: $1,000 (allocated across leads)
Total Marketing: $2,500

Sales Spend (per customer):
- AE salary: $1,200 (assumes 10 deals/year per AE)
- SDR salary: $800 (assumes 15 deals/year per SDR)
- SE (Solutions Engineer): $500 (POC support)
Total Sales: $2,500

Total CAC: $5,000 per customer
```

**CAC Payback Period:**
```
Average contract: $60K year 1 (blended Professional + Enterprise)
Gross margin: 85%
Contribution margin: $51K

Payback: $5,000 / ($51K / 12 months) = 1.2 months ✅

Target: <6 months (best-in-class SaaS)
Sutra: ~1 month (exceptional)
```

---

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**LTV Calculation:**
```
Average Annual Contract Value (ACV): $60,000
Gross Margin: 85%
Annual Contribution: $51,000

Customer Lifetime:
- Churn rate: 5% annually
- Average lifetime: 1 / 0.05 = 20 years (but discount to 5 years for conservatism)

LTV (Conservative): $51K × 5 years = $255,000

LTV (Optimistic, 10 years): $51K × 10 years = $510,000
```

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
```
Conservative: $255K / $5K = 51:1 ✅
Optimistic: $510K / $5K = 102:1 ✅

Target: >3:1 (acceptable SaaS)
Great: >5:1
Exceptional: >10:1

Sutra: 51:1 (exceptional) - driven by low churn, high retention
```

---

**Revenue Components:**


## Pricing Optimization Strategies

### Price Testing Framework

**Hypothesis:** Enterprise tier underpriced at $120K/year

**Test Plan:**
1. A/B test new customers: $150K vs $120K
2. Measure close rate, sales cycle length
3. If close rate >40% at $150K → permanent increase
4. Expected outcome: +25% ACV, -10% close rate = +12.5% revenue

**Annual Pricing Reviews:**
- Increase prices 10-15% annually (inflation + value adds)
- Grandfather existing customers (1-year grace period)
- New customers on new pricing immediately

---
